whats new

  • New School Swimming Pool

    The new swimming pool was created last summer. A big pool to practice for the inter-school competition and for professional swimming in a full sized full to help us learn it better. Now we know that how much big is a real pool and how to swim properly in it. In this pool now we could know how pools in competition look and how do we do our best and win a competition in this pool. The shade in the pool helps in aavoiding direct sun in the eyes of the swimmer. It also helps absorb heat so we can swim even if it is a little bit cold. The pool is not heated but the shade is made in a way to absorb the heat that comes on it.

  • New School Library

    The new library is created to encourage us to read more and understand more. To help make our vocabulary rich and to help us speak in english with more of a flow fluently. We can take books from the library to read but we need return it back in 2 weeks or then we have to pay the due fees. The library is making us read more which will help us in the future. The reading is also helping us in getting to know more words and difficult words. The library is a very silent place and a good place for reading. We can focus more on the book than the noises in the sorrundings.
